The Best K-Drama According To Sonya

My Top 3 K-drama's of all time.

Crash Landing on You

The idea of a South Korean heiress crash-landing in North Korea was fresh and daring. It offered a rare and romanticized look into a forbidden, politically sensitive setting.
It seamlessly combined romance, comedy, action, and drama where
emotional moments were balanced with humor and suspense.

Descendents of the Sun

The central love story between Captain Yoo Si-jin and Dr. Kang Mo-yeon was full of emotional tension, charm, and mutual respect.
It tackled themes like duty vs. desire, sacrifice, and moral responsibility, creating emotional depth and raising the stakes of the romance.
